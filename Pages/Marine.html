Overview

Marine is a comprehensive insurance policy that covers goods, freight and other interests against loss or damage whilst being transported by rail, road,
sea and/or air from the port of loading to the final destination.

It is ideal for both individuals and corporates transporting goods inland, importing or exporting like manufacturers, wholesalers, miners and producers.

Marine covers you for:

1. For air and sea freight.
2. For bulk consignments covered for maritime perils: fire, theft, sinking, collision, jettison of cargo, general average sacrifice, pilferage, total loss of vessel.
3. Guaranteed out-turn (GOT) available for palm and petroleum products.
4. Enjoy personalized customer service and timely claim settlement.
